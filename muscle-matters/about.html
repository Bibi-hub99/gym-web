Muscle Matters

Muscle Matters is a south african based gym complex for providing gym equipments,services and needs of the gym. Founded in 2010, the company has been operating all over the world, bringing innovations and providing affordable services to clients.the company began as a gym for gym lovers to visit and feel at home without hidden and overpaying in fees. the company has devoted itself as a must have for gym enthusiasts and our goal lies in being number gym organization in the whole by not overpricing clients but try to provide solutions at a very affordable price. Muscle Matters is strategically positioned and located in place where it can a massive role in encouraging gym life and reduce costs for clients Approximately 100 000 clients visit us and our franchises.

we adhere to bringing the best experience to gym lovers and fans with our top notch services delivered with ease and love for you.we primary serve and align with the interests of the poor and working class all over the world who otherwise will not have easy access to gym services and equipments needed.because of our affordability and availability we have decided to act as a gateway for huge population from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Founder
